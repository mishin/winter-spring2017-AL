User FAQ

▾ How do I add a minor?
First, click the add minor button. The default minor classes will populate into the flow chart.
▾ How do I edit a flowchart?
To start editing your flowchart, click the edit button on the menu. Drag and drop the classes to the necessary locations. Click the save button and your editing session will end. If you would like to print your flowchart, click the print button on the side.
▾ How do I make a note?
Click on the class you would like to make a note on. Within the popup box, click on add note. Write what you need in the note. Click the Add Note button and your editing session will end Your new note will be displayed whenever you click on that class. The course will have a visual cue showing that the note has been added.
▾ How do I display the course information?
Click on the course of the information you would like to see. A popup window will show up with the course name, course description, prerequisites, and term the course is typically offered. Once finished, click anywhere outside of the box and the popup box will disappear.
▾ If I have changed my major, how do I change my flowchart to represent my new major?
First, select the “Edit Profile” option in the sidebar of your home screen. In the editing page, change your major selection to represent your new major and save your profile. Select the option “Add New Flowchart”. You will know have a flowchart populated with courses related to your new major.
▾ How do I mark a course as complete?
Click the edit button on the sidebar to enter editing mode for the flowchart. Select the course you would like to mark in your flowchart. A box with options should appear on selection. Choose to mark the course as complete. Click the save button and your editing session will end.

Administrator FAQ

▾ How do I edit a course?
Select the course you would like to edit. Click the edit button on the sidebar to edit the course. You will be taken to an edit screen where the course information can be changed. Click the save button and your editing session will end.
▾ How do I edit a flowchart?
Select the flowchart you would like to edit. Click the edit button on the sidebar to edit the flowchart. You will be taken to an edit screen where the flowchart can be changed. Click the save button and your editing session will end.
▾ Can I view a collection of the user data?
Yes. On the home screen of the SLOChart page, there will be an option in the sidebar to view this information.
